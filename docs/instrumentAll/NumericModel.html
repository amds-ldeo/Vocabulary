Concepts

1.1 Numerical model

  • Categories used in the SeaDataNet project to classify algorithmic predications that produce datasets.
  • Alternate labels: SeaDataNet numerical model categories, numerical models,
  • Concept URI: http://vocab.nerc.ac.uk/collection/L21/current/ICAT08

1.1.1 Global models

  • Child of: ICAT08
  • Numerical models that make predictions over the whole globe.
  • Concept URI: http://vocab.nerc.ac.uk/collection/L05/current/MOD01

1.1.2 Regional models

  • Child of: ICAT08
  • Numerical models that make predictions within a limited geographic area.
  • Concept URI: http://vocab.nerc.ac.uk/collection/L05/current/MOD02

1.1.3 Atmosphere models

  • Child of: ICAT08
  • Numerical models the make predictions in the atmosphere assuming that it is a closed system that doesn’t interact with the hydrosphere.
  • Concept URI: http://vocab.nerc.ac.uk/collection/L05/current/MOD03

1.1.4 Ocean models

  • Child of: ICAT08
  • Numerical models the make predictions in the hydrosphere assuming that it is a closed system that doesn’t interact with the atmosphere.
  • Concept URI: http://vocab.nerc.ac.uk/collection/L05/current/MOD04

1.1.5 Coupled models

  • Child of: ICAT08
  • Numerical models that make predictions in both the atmosphere and the hydrosphere by considering both spheres as a single system.
  • Concept URI: http://vocab.nerc.ac.uk/collection/L05/current/MOD05

1.1.6 Meteorological models

  • Child of: ICAT08
  • Numerical models that make predictions of weather parameters such as air temperature, pressure, humidity and winds.
  • Concept URI: http://vocab.nerc.ac.uk/collection/L05/current/MOD06

1.1.7 Physical oceanographic models

  • Child of: ICAT08
  • Numerical models that make predictions of physical oceanographic parameters such as water body temperature, salinity, currents and waves.
  • Concept URI: http://vocab.nerc.ac.uk/collection/L05/current/MOD07

1.1.8 Biological and biogeochemical models

  • Child of: ICAT08
  • Numerical models that make predictions of biological parameters, including ecosystem models.
  • Concept URI: http://vocab.nerc.ac.uk/collection/L05/current/MOD08
